Website Development Process

01

Discovery

We begin by defining your project’s goals, scope, timeline, and resources. This phase includes in-depth information gathering — collecting both business and technical requirements

02

Information Architecture

We organize the structure and hierarchy of your website for easy navigation and user understanding. This involves creating sitemaps, user flows, and content maps that storyboard how the site will function.

03

Web design

Using the structure, we create wireframes and visual layouts that align with your brand and business goals. This step focuses on user interface (UI) and user experience (UX), ensuring that your design not only looks great but also converts and engages effectively.

04

Content

We prepare and organize all the necessary website content — including text, images, videos, and downloads. Content is optimized for the search engine , readability, and audience engagement.

05

Web development

Here’s where your website comes to life. We convert the approved designs into a functioning website, building the frontend and backend as needed. This is done in a development environment, allowing for testing, feedback, and fine-tuning before launch.

06

Testing

We rigorously test your website to ensure it meets all functional and non-functional requirements. This ensures your site meets or exceeds industry standards before going live.

07

Deployment

After testing and final approvals, your website is launched to the live environment. This may involve domain setup, hosting, server configuration, and deployment pipelines. Your site is now accessible to your audience.

08

Maintaince & Support

After launch, we continue to monitor, update, and optimize your website. This includes fixing bugs, updating content, applying security patches, and making performance improvements — ensuring your site continues to perform at its best.
